When you run this command Hesa will send one large message containing the text you want "spammed" however many times you want.
Due to Discord limitations the number of chars in you message multiplied by the amount of times it will be "spammed" can't exceed 4000.

*Usage:*
`hesa spam [amount]; [text]`

*Examples:*
`hesa spam 100; Hello` will send one large message containing "Hello", 100 times
