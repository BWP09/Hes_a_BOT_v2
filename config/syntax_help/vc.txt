This command will play the specified file in the connected voice channel. You can also pause, resume, and stop the playback.
It can also list all available and playable files.

*Usage:*
`hesa vc <"play"; [file name], "pause", "resume", "stop", "list">`

*Examples:*
`hesa vc play; melvin.mp4` will play "melvin.mp4" in the connected VC
`hesa vc list` will send a list of playable files
